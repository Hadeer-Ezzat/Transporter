ABOUT US

Established in 2014, Transporter Logistics & Courier Services is a fast growing courier and integrated express package Distribution Company based in Cairo, Egypt. From the believe of having an Egyptian courier service that is professional, timely and adding value to customers, the idea of founding Transporter Logistics & Courier Services came in 2014.

Ahmed Abbas, Fady Gad and Ahmed Keshk, the co-founders of Transporter Logistics& Courier Services, took the risk of establishing the company with the great trust of the importance of having such a service that will serve not only the customers in Cairo, but also will expand to serve all governorates in Egypt.
